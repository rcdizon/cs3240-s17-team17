About

Lokahi Fintech is a financial technology company that will bring you into the future of finance. Whether you are looking to invest or looking to sell, Lokahi will show you how to become part of a greater community of individuals who help keep the market moving. Through our services, you will be able to send messages to other companies and investors, file reports and upload files, search through recent reports filed by other companies, and become part of a community of companies and investors.
